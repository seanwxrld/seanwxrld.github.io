DEBUT ALBUM

TWĒNTY

SĒAN MOSIKILI · Expected April 10, 2026

TWĒNTY is an upcoming deeply introspective debut album by SĒAN MOSIKILI, a talented singer-songwriter and record producer, that captures the emotional, psychological, and spiritual weight of coming of age. Written at the starting point of his twenties, this project explores themes of self-worth, love, vulnerability, ambition, and inner conflict, unfolding as a personal diary rather than a performative statement.

Sonically, TWĒNTY blends contemporary dark pop, alt R&B, alternative soul, and minimalist pop production, marked by restrained arrangements, emotive vocals, and atmospheric textures. Each track functions as a chapter, tracing the evolution from emotional confusion and imbalance to clarity, boundaries, and self-acceptance. The album's tone is raw yet refined, prioritizing honesty over polish and emotional resonance over excess.

With TWĒNTY, SĒAN MOSIKILI positions himself as an artist driven by storytelling, emotional precision, and intentional artistry. The album is not only a reflection on youth but also a statement of identity — documenting the moment where growth begins to outweigh uncertainty.
