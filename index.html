LOST LOVER'S AVENUE

SĒAN MOSIKILI · OUT FRIDAY, 12 JUNE 2026

LOST LOVER'S AVENUE is the official debut album by SĒAN MOSIKILI — A pop, alt-R&B, and pop-rock world built for late-night overthinkers, dreamers, and emotional survivors.

Across seven tracks, it explores fame obsession, toxic attachment, loneliness, escapism through nightlife, and healing through self-awareness.
