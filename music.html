TWĒNTY (Album)

TWĒNTY is an upcoming deeply introspective debut album by SĒAN MOSIKILI. Written at the starting point of his twenties, the project explores themes of self-worth, love, vulnerability, ambition, and inner conflict, unfolding as a personal diary rather than a performative statement.

Sonically, TWĒNTY blends contemporary dark pop, alt R&B, alternative soul, and minimalist pop production, marked by restrained arrangements, emotive vocals, and atmospheric textures. Expected April 10, 2026.
